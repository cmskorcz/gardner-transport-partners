Mission Statement

Gardner Transport Partners is Michigan's premier Dump-Truck service provider with specialization in land work, excavation, grading and hauling of materials for commercial and residential projects. Our dispatch team allows us to connect developers and home owners with owner operators looking for work in South East Michigan. This, in turn, allows a path for us to reach our goal: to build a stronger Michigan!

About Us

Gardner Transport Partners has grown to be able to provide a variety of services in the Metro Detroit area, but we are owner operators at heart with a passion for Dump-Trucks and the construction that will rebuild Michigan. From commercial developments to home improvements, we are dedicated to help facilitate the revitalization of Michigan's infrastructure.

Built from the ground up, we started with one truck and trailer and have built GTP's operation to service a wide variety of construction-based projects. Our teams specializing in: excavation, grading, general land work, storm clean-up, demolition and of course, hauling and dumping.
